Poetry Booklet

Typesetting · EKA student project · 2024

A poetry booklet featuring 8 poems by Estonian poet Tõnis Vilu alongside two related articles. The poems are typeset into a sound wave form inspired by their spoken, performative quality, creating a completely new rhythm of reading. The articles are set in simple justified columns so they don't compete with the central form of the poems.

A4. Supervised by Ott Kagovere, 2024.
