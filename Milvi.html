Typeface "Milvi"

Type Design · Glyphs · 2026

The core idea behind this font came from the nature and atmosphere of my grandmother's garden. The spiraling and wavy forms illustrate the vines and flowers found there, while the overall fairytale-like quality captures the beauty and calm of that space. The letters were hand-drawn and later traced in Glyphs and it's lowercase letters are still a work in progress. Supervized by Celine Hurka.
