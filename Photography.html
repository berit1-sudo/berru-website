Photography · Personal project · 2022–2026

A selection of photographs taken in the streets of Tallinn and Rakvere and at snowboard competitions and sessions across Estonia, shot on a Sony A7.

Most of these photos are of close friends and local riders who have shaped how I see snowboarding. Photography is a way of giving something back to a community that has given a lot, and of holding onto the moments that matter before they are gone.
