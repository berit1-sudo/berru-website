Color Bingo in Estonian Academy of Arts

Graphic design · EKA Student project · 2025

Color Bingo was created for our Typography III course exploring typography in public spaces. The project consisted of two parts: designing promotional posters as a temporary publication, and hosting the Color Bingo event as a larger-scale public space intervention.

This project came to life with my coursemates Mia Greta Sepp and Elisabeth Mägi. Held on October 30th as part of EKA's birthday programme, Color Bingo was a game night built around the idea that color is everywhere if you actually stop to look. With over 100 players, the event was a success. At the end of the course, a small publication was made documenting the process of the project.
